# Official Olympia Bar League Rulebook (2025 Season)

## 1. Scoring Mechanics
* **Check-In:** 10 Points. Requires geolocation verification within 50m of the venue.
* **Vibe Report:** 5 Points. Requires uploading a photo of the current atmosphere.
* **League Night Bonus:** 1.5x Multiplier on points earned during designated events (e.g., Tuesday Trivia).

## 2. The "Hat Trick" (Velocity Limit)
* You may check into a maximum of **3 venues** per operational night (6 AM to 2 AM).
* **Cool-down:** You must wait **45 minutes** between check-ins at different venues.
* *Why?* To encourage enjoying the venue, not just sprinting for points.

## 3. Disqualification
* Any use of GPS spoofers, emulators, or account sharing will result in an immediate Season Ban.
* Harassment of venue staff or other players results in a permanent platform ban.
