# OlyBars Glossary: The League Ecosystem

## Core Entities

### **The League (Olympia Bar League)**
The overarching governing body and gamification layer. It is not a separate club you go to; it is the *network* of all participating venues.

### **League Member (The Venue)**
* **Definition:** A business (Bar, Pub, Arcade) that is an official partner of the platform.
* **Status:** All listed venues on OlyBars.com are League Members. They pay dues (subscription) and host League Events.
* **Role:** They provide the "Arena" (the space) and the "Gear" (the games).

### **Player (The User)**
* **Definition:** A registered user on OlyBars.com.
* **Action:** You "Join the League" by creating an account.
* **Goal:** Earn **League Points (LP)** by participating in the real world.

## Vibe Check Terminology

### **Vibe Scale**
* **Dead:** Empty. Good for reading a book or private conversations.
* **Chill:** Relaxed. Space to sit, low noise, easy service.
* **Buzzing:** Active. The standard "Friday Night" energy. Good music, talking required. (Replaces old "Lively" and "Buzzing").
* **Packed:** Shoulder-to-shoulder. High energy, loud, "Party Mode."

### **Game Status**
* **Open:** The table/machine is free to use right now.
* **Taken:** Someone is currently playing.
* **Down / Needs Repair:** The machine is eating quarters or the flipper is stuck. (Reported by Players).

## Bonus Mechanics

### **First Check-in Bonus**
* **Definition:** Extra LP awarded to the *very first* player to Vibe Check a specific venue that operational day.
* **Goal:** Encourages early data population so other users know what's happening.

### **Hot Spot (Boost)**
* **Definition:** A temporary multiplier applied to a specific venue (e.g., "Double Points at Hannah's tonight").
* **Trigger:** Activated by Admin (OlyBars) or purchased by the Venue to drive traffic.
